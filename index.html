But who is Michaela Perez?

In short, I am a Computer Engineering junior at The University of Texas at Dallas from San Antonio, TX. I am a follower in Christ who believes that people need to fundamentally learn to love one another in order to find true peace in life. I love learning and-- even more than that-- sharing what I learn. I pride myself on my work ethic and cannot help but be an initiator in every project I commit myself to. Some more things about me:

  • I want to use the skills I'm learning in the biomedical field
  • I enjoy research but want more experience in industry
  • I'm fascinated by project management and understanding people better
  • I love bees, avocados, Tejano music, and Huevos Rancheros in SATX cafes on Sunday mornings with my mom
